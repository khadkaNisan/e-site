About HamroBazaarNepal

Bringing Nepal's diverse products to your doorstep since 2022

Our Story

Founded in 2022, HamroBazaarNepal.com was created by a team of passionate Nepali entrepreneurs with a vision to digitize Nepal's rich shopping culture.

We connect customers with trusted sellers across Nepal – from the bustling markets of Kathmandu to the handmade crafts of Bhaktapur and organic farms of Kaski.

Our platform makes it easy to discover authentic Nepali products while supporting local businesses and artisans throughout the country.

We're more than just an eCommerce platform – we're a digital extension of Nepal's vibrant marketplace tradition, bringing the warmth and diversity of Nepali bazaars to your fingertips.
